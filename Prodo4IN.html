Strong Beauty

The most acclaimed and at the same time most criticized work of the author, created opinions divided from his sample to the public in 2002, even being criticized by the most respected master of Arts in Bikini Bottom citing; -Strong beauty, this? . . . Or rather "It belongs to the garbage" -.However, this fact only motivated the artist to move forward to demonstrate what his work is worth.

Artist: Squidward Tentacles.
Size: 91 cm × 74 cm.
Location: Bikini Bottom Arts School.
Technique: Oil, tempera and cake on cardboard.
Year: 2002
Style: Surrealism.
